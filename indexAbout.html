About us

"Italian Lovers World", by Ruggero Zanon and Luca Zanatta, offers an enogastronomic trip to discover typical Venetian cuisine, through the choice of Italian raw materials, fresh products and in the respect of our precious culinary tradition.

Reservations by email will be taken into account before 16:00. If you wish to book the same day before 18:00 please call +32 486 53 95 97, and +32 2 512 34 47 after 18:00. Thank you for your understanding.

Born in 2003, this restaurant offers a combination of a home-style cooking and a careful research of the details, in a warm and welcoming environment.

The restaurant is located in the heart of the city of Brussels, bringing a corner of Italy within walking distance of the Grand-Place and the Manneken-Pis.

On request it is also possible to organize private events.
